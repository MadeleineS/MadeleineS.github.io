Arts and Crafts

Yancey and Mitchell counties have a vibrant arts community, and is also close to Ashville 's arts scene. You can visit studios, galleries and attend multiple events throughout the year.

  • River Arts Council (TRAC)
  • Penland School of Crafts
  • Spruce Pine Potters Market
  • Highway 80 Art Hop
  • River Arts District Ashville

Outdoors

The Black Mountains range and Mount Mitchell, Roan Mountain, Grand Father Mountain, Linville Gorge, the Toe river are all within a 35 miles radius from Mountain brooks, as well as a large stretch of the Blue Ridge Parkway. This region offers endless outdoor activities: hiking, from short walks (stroller and wheel chair accessible) to challenging day or multi-day hikes, fishing, tubing or rafting, picking berries in the late summer, riding ATV, etc.
In the winter, you can get to the ski slopes of Sugar Mountain , Beech Mountain, or Wolf Laurel in just over an hour.
