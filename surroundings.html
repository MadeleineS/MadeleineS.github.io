Nestled in the western North Carolina mountains Mountain Brook Estates offers easy access to area activities for everyone, from outdoor activities to the arts.

Arts and Crafts

Yancey and Mitchell counties have a vibrant arts community, and are also close to Ashville 's arts scene. You can visit studios, galleries and attend multiple events throughout the year.

  • Toe River Arts Council (TRAC)
  • Penland School of Crafts
  • Spruce Pine Potters Market
  • Highway 80 Art Hop
  • River Arts District Ashville

Outdoors

The Black Mountains range and Mount Mitchell, Roan Mountain, Grandfather Mountain, Linville Gorge, the Toe river are all within a 35 miles radius from Mountain Brook, as well as a large stretch of the Blue Ridge Parkway. This region offers endless outdoor activities: hiking, from short walks (stroller and wheel chair accessible) to challenging day or multi-day hikes, fishing, tubing or rafting, picking berries in the late summer, riding ATV, etc.
In the winter, you can get to the ski slopes of Sugar Mountain , Beech Mountain, or Wolf Laurel in just over an hour.


Other Points of interest

  • Gem mining
  • Biltmore Estates and the Arboretum in Ashville
  • Many beautiful waterfalls
